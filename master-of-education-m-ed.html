Master of Education (M.Ed.)

Secondary Teacher’s Training Course (B.Ed.)

Two year training programme, for shaping a graduate into a Secondary Teacher. The college is affiliated to Saurashtra University, Rajkot and approved by Government of Gujarat.
National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), New Delhi, has recognized both the teacher training programmes conduct by this college.

Masters of Education (M.Ed.)

Two year programme, for initiating research skills in Teacher Education.
Approval received from NCTE because our B.Ed. programme was Re-Accredited with B Grade (2.76 CGPA) by NCCA. The course is affiliated with Saurashtra University, Rajkot
